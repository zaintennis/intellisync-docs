**Intellisync Solutions** Web App is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline and enhance the way you manage your workflows and communications. Built on modern web technologies, Intellisync Solutions offers a user-friendly interface for efficient management of your tasks and projects. 
This guide will help you get started with setting up and using the web app.

## Create your account

To create your own account on Intellisync, follow these steps:
- Visit the [Intellisync](https://intellisyncsolutions.io/) website.
- Click on signup if you already do not have a account. Use login to enter using your pre-existing account
- Follow the prompts to create your new account and follow the onboarding steps.

<Callout variant="info">
Try creating an account in your browser: [Signup to IntelliSync](https://intellisyncsolutions.io/auth?signup=false)
</Callout>

## Create your assistant
Create a new chatbot. Click on “Create Chatbot” button.

Give your chatbot a name. Click on the “Create” button.

## Customizing Your Profile
You have full control over the identity and look of your assistant.

Edit chatbot profile image and name. Click to edit chatbot profile.
