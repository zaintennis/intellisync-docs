title: Settings

The **Settings** page allows you to customize and manage various aspects of your assistant. This is where you can fine-tune its appearance, behavior, and accessibility. Here's a quick overview of what you can do in the Settings:

## Edit Assistant

The **Edit Assistant** section lets you modify the basic information and appearance of your assistant. You can:

- Change the assistant's name.
- Upload a profile photo that will be displayed as your assistant's avatar.
- Choose the appearance mode for your assistant, such as Automatic, Dark, or Light.
- Set conversation starters that will appear as prompts for users to begin interactions.

## Share

The **Share** section provides options to make your assistant accessible to others. You can:

- Set the assistant's privacy to **Private** or **Public**.
- Generate a shareable link to allow others to interact with your assistant.
- Obtain an embed code to integrate your assistant into a website.
